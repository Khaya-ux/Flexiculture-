Shipping & Refund Policy

Shipping Policy

At Flexiculture, we are committed to delivering your orders quickly and efficiently. Please review our shipping policies below:

  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 5-7 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Shipping Rates: Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on your location.
  • Delivery Time: Estimated delivery times vary by location but typically range from 2-6 business days.
  • Order Tracking: Once shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.
  • International Shipping: We currently do not ship internationally.

If you experience any shipping delays, please contact us at support@flexiculture.com.

Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not happy with your purchase, please review our refund policy:

  • Eligibility for Refunds: Items must be returned within 1-2 days of delivery, unused and in their original packaging.
  • Non-Refundable Items: Customized or sale items are not eligible for refunds.
  • Return Process: To initiate a return, contact us at support@flexiculture.com with your order number and reason for return.
  • Refund Processing: Refunds are processed within 2-4 days after the return is received and inspected.

Privacy Policy

At Flexiculture, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

  • Information We Collect: Name, email, phone number, address, payment details.
  • How We Use Your Information: To process orders, provide customer support, and improve our services.
  • Third-Party Services: We do not sell or share your data with third parties except for payment processing and delivery partners.
  • Data Security: We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies.

For any privacy concerns, contact us at support@flexiculture.com.
